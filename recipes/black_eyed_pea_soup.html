Black-Eyed pea soup

Description

This is a great New Year's meal! Perfect on a cold winter night. Great comfort food. Serve with corn bread.

Cooking time around 1 hour and prep time of 15 minutes bringing the total time for making the dish to 1 hour and 15 minutes
